MINNESOTA INVESTIGATION

The $9 Billion Scandal

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Between 2020-2025, Minnesota experienced one of the largest documented Medicaid fraud scandals in U.S. history—a $9+ billion theft revealing comprehensive systemic failure across criminal, political, bureaucratic, judicial, and oversight systems simultaneously.

The Numbers:

  • Total Documented Fraud: $9 billion+
  • Total Estimated (including MCO): $28 billion+
  • Convicted: 100+
  • Recovered: <$70 million
  • Recovery Rate: <1%
  • Whistleblowers Retaliated: 480+

THE FRAUD MECHANISM

Channel 1: Direct DHS Payments (20%)

Feeding Our Future: $250M-$350M stolen through fake nonprofits claiming to serve meals to children. 250+ shell entities submitted fraudulent reimbursement requests with no actual meal service.

Channel 2: MCO Capitation (80%)

The critical blindspot: $8 billion annually flows through MCOs with zero independent audit. MCOs audit themselves. Estimated MCO fraud: $5-10 billion.

THE OVERSEAS MONEY TRAIL

  • China: $1.1M+ (unrecoverable) - Najmo Ahmed to textile companies
  • Somalia: $1M+ (terror investigation) - Abdiaziz Farah; Treasury investigating Al-Shabaab links
  • Kenya: $3M+ (partially traceable) - Ahmednaji Sheikh indicted
  • Maldives: $500K+ (unrecoverable) - Abdimajid Nur honeymoon transfers

THE MCO AUDIT BLINDSPOT

Governor Walz hired Optum (UnitedHealth subsidiary) as auditor. Optum audit covers only 20% of Medicaid. The remaining 80% ($8B annually) through MCOs has zero independent oversight.
