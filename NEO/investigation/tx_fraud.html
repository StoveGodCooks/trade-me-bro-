TEXAS INVESTIGATION

97% Through MCOs, 5-15% Recovery Rate

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Texas replicates the identical fraud patterns, oversight failures, and political protection mechanisms documented in Minnesota and California, with an estimated $4 billion in annual fraud losses against a paltry 5-15% recovery rate.

The state's $41 billion Medicaid managed care system—channeling 97% of beneficiaries through 16 MCOs—demonstrates the same structural vulnerabilities enabling organized fraud networks to extract billions while political connections shield both fraudsters and negligent MCOs from accountability.

MCO OVERSIGHT COLLAPSE

CMS July 2025 Review Found:

  • All 4 major MCOs failed to conduct unannounced site visits
  • No SIU staffing ratios to member population
  • No beneficiary verification requirements
  • HHSC-OIG doesn't track MCO-to-MFCU referrals
  • Deficient corrective action plan policies

Overpayment Recovery Rates:

  • Amerigroup: Identified $26.8M, recovered $5.5M (20.5%)
  • Superior HealthPlan: Identified $13.6M, recovered $3.1M (22.8%)
  • ~80% of identified overpayments go unrecovered

INSTITUTIONAL FAILURES

MCO Special Investigative Units

Wellpoint Texas (processing $12.8B in claims) recovered only $426,467—a recovery rate of 0.003%.

State Auditor Findings

Evaluation errors in all 28 procurements totaling $4.6 billion. For 5 procurements exceeding $3B, auditor couldn't confirm HHSC awarded contracts to best applicants.

Federal HHS-OIG Findings

Texas failed to correctly report $24.3 million for 65 MFCU cases. For 26 cases, Texas simply did not report $19.0 million in overpayments.

RECOVERY RATES

COMPARATIVE MATRIX

Texas replicates every structural failure documented in Minnesota and California:

  • 97% Medicaid through MCOs (vs 80%+ in MN/CA)
  • MCO fraud referral deficiencies (CMS confirmed)
  • Site visit requirements ignored (all 4 MCOs failed)
  • Low overpayment recovery (20-23%)
  • Political donations from MCOs
  • Contract awards despite fraud settlements
  • International money flows
  • Fraud recovery rate <10%
  • Revolving door (21CT scandal)
  • COVID waiver abuse
