2. THE MONTE VISTA PHARMACY FRAUD

Timeline: May 2022 – March 2023 (10 months)

Amount Stolen: $204,032,151

How It Worked: DHCS suspended prior authorization during Medi-Cal Rx transition. Monte Vista exploited this by:

  • Billing for expensive drugs without proving medical necessity
  • Many prescriptions never actually dispensed
  • Kickbacks of up to 40% paid to co-schemers
  • Billing "tens of millions per month" for single pharmacy
